Hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) is a common clinical malignant disease and the second leading cause of cancer-related death worldwide. Dendrobium is a commonly applied nourishing drug in traditional Chinese medicine. Gigantol is a phenolic compound extracted from Dendrobium. The compound has attracted attention for its anticancer effects. However, the mechanism of gigantol in HCC has not been extensively explored.
