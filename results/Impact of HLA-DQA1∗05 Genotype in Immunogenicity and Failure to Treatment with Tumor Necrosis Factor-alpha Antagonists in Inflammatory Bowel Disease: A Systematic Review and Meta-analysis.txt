HLA-DQA1*05 carriage has been associated with an increased risk of immunogenicity in patients with immune-mediated inflammatory diseases treated with tumor necrosis factor-alpha (TNF-a) antagonists. Results have shown an inconsistent association with a loss of response (LOR) in patients with inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), which could be modified when using proactive optimization and association with immunomodulatory drugs.
