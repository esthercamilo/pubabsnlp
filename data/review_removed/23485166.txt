Latest trends in preventive medicine: highlights of the PreMed 2008 meeting, 6 - 7 June 2008, Monza, Italy.
The PreMed symposium is dedicated to presenting the potential contribution of laboratory medicine to preventive medicine and to the prediction of diseases. At the 2008 PreMed meeting, innovative research approaches, new technologies for laboratory medicine, late-breaking news for molecular medicine and new approaches for disease screening were presented by top scientists.
