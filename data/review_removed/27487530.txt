[Modern look at pharmacotherapy of osteoarthritis accomplished with cardiovascular disease].
ABSTR ACTCT Introduction: At present, the main pathogenetic link in the development of OA as atherosclerosis is chronic inflammation, which is based on the activation of pro-inflammatory mediators and disorders of cholesterol metabolism. The aim of the research was to study the efficiency of the use of rosuvastatin in the treatment of patients with osteoarthritis in the combined arterial disease.
