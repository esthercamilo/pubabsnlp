How to Control Asthma with Personalized Management: Where Do We Stand Now?
Asthma is one of the most significant diseases worldwide and causes overwhelming costs physically and economically. The study of asthma has revealed various groups of asthma patients who share phenotypic characteristics that naturally elicit the need for personalized asthma therapy. An increasing amount of pharmacogenetics research, genotype-based trials and precision medicine trials have been conducted to investigate this problem.
