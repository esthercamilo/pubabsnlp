Pharmacogenetic study of ACE, AGT, CYP11B1, CYP11B2 and eNOS gene variants in hypertensive patients from Faisalabad, Pakistan.
To investigate the association of genetic variants of renin angiotensin aldosterone system, endothelial nitric oxide synthase and 11-beta-hydroxylase genes, and the drug efficacy of angiotensin-converting enzyme inhibitor and angiotensin receptor blocker.
