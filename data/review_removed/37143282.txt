Application of Network Pharmacology and Molecular Docking to Explore the Mechanism of Danggui Liuhuang Tang against Hyperthyroidism.
To investigate the mechanism of Danggui Liuhuang Tang (DGLHT) in the treatment of hyperthyroidism (HT), we explored the multi-component, multi-target, and multi-pathway mechanism based on the network pharmacology method of traditional Chinese medicine.
