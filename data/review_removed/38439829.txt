Frequencies of HLA-B alleles in Indonesian Malay Ethnic.
The <i>HLA-B</i> alleles have been used as a marker to predict drug-induced adverse reactions and as a major contributor to hypersensitivity reactions. We examined the feasibility of <i>HLA-B</i> alleles as pharmacogenomic markers of drug-induced hypersensitivity in an Indonesian Malay Ethnic.
