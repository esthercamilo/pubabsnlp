Antihypertensive therapy in pre-eclampsia: effects of plasma from nonresponsive patients on endothelial gene expression.
Over 40% of patients with pre-eclampsia are nonresponsive to antihypertensive therapy, but the underlying mechanisms are unknown. We examined the effects of plasma from nonresponsive and responsive patients on endothelial gene expression.
