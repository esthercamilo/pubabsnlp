Urine metabolic ratio of omeprazole in relation to CYP2C19 polymorphisms in Russian peptic ulcer patients.
CYP2C19 is known to be the main enzyme of biotransformation of proton pump inhibitors (PPIs), whereas the <i>CYP2C19</i> gene is highly polymorphic. Genotyping and phenotyping together represent more reliable data about patient's CYP2C19 activity.
