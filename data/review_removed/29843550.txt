The polymorphisms of growth factor genes (VEGFA & EGF) were associated with response to acitretin in psoriasis.
VEGF and EGF are assumed to be involved in the pathogenesis of psoriasis, while the impacts of their polymorphisms on psoriasis are inconsistent. Therefore, we hope to clarify these relationships in the Chinese Han population.
